Welcome to

KinnCare

KinnCare represents a departure from standard American health care.

We are a full service, drugless, natural health care clinic. We combine hands-on-chiropractic care, comprehensive naturopathic medicine, physical therapy, cutting edge technology, and specialized software systems to provide the highest possible level of service to our patients. Whether you are in pain, sick, tired, or feeling great, our office will deliver personalized healthcare at its best.

You will enjoy the latest top-of-the-line rehabilitation and treatment devices; receive genuine attention from our doctors and staff; find treatment options unavailable in other doctors offices; and gain access to realistic and affordable wellness, preventive, and treatment services.

Our private health care practice was designed with you in mind, because,

  • You recognize quality.

  • You prefer to avoid drugs and surgery.

  • You are health-conscious.

  • You understand the body is self-healing.

We understand the problems in health care and the importance of natural, drugless, non surgical health solutions. We believe good health isn't prescribed; it's created from within. So we've designed a truly natural and holistic practice to support your goals – because your goals are our goals.

Sincerely yours in health,

Dr. John F. Koloski, DC and Dr. Erin E. Kinney, ND
